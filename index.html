Change check

Before and after deployment

Use payment, database, outage, and recovery traffic to explain how a release can change error rate, latency, logs, alerts, and SLO health.

  1. Start with symptom: generate payment, database, or outage traffic from the simulator.
  2. Check metrics: open the metrics tab and compare endpoints by errors, traffic, and latency.
  3. Check logs: filter by severity : "error", status >= 500, or duration_ms > 500.
  4. Check traces: open Jaeger for the slow operation and inspect the listed dependency.
  5. Check SLO: confirm whether availability, latency compliance, or burn rate moved.
  6. Check deployments: decide whether recent release impact matches the error or latency pattern.
